ABOUT ME

I am an abstract and impressionist painter from Laurens, South Carolina. Studying at Presbyterian College in Clinton, South Carolina, I graduated with Bachelor of Arts in the Fine Arts in 2012.

My inspiration comes from natural landscapes and the visual fragments of cultures and society. I enjoy experimenting with discreet and bold hues on canvas and watching the raw, unpolished finishes they create.

If you see a piece that interests you or have questions about a custom piece , please contact me to learn more.
